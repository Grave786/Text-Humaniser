FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about modes, passes, and access.

What do the modes do?

Balanced targets best quality, Fast is optimized for long text, and Stealth adds more variation.

Why multiple passes?

More passes can push the writing further from the original draft (slower, but often more natural).

Who can create accounts?

Admins create and manage user accounts. There are no public self-signups.

Where do I see my past runs?

Open the Profile panel in the workspace to view recent logs (up to 50).

Best practices

Use 2-6 short paragraphs for the most natural results.

Quick workflow

Paste -> Humanize -> Review -> Copy.

Need admin access?

Contact your administrator to get an account provisioned.
